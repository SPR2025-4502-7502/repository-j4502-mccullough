A Dog's Life

A visual journey of my 14-year-old dog Gabby's life from puppy years to her final years. This gallery contrasts a dog's perspective of a fulfilling lifetime with their human family's perspective of their all-too-short time on earth. Each image captures the beautiful moments shared between dog and human, displaying flashbacks paused in time that were full of love, play, and unforgettable memories.
